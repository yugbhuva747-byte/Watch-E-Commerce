--Our Story--

Born from a
Passion for
Perfection

LUXWATCH was founded by a trio of horological obsessives who believed India deserved a luxury watch brand rooted in local artistry and global craftsmanship.

Our Journey

A Decade of Excellence

2013

The Workshop Opens

Three watchmakers, one rented loft in Surat. Our first collection — twelve pieces — sold out in three days.

2016

Swiss Partnership

Our alliance with ETA Geneva brought Swiss mechanical movements — without compromising our Indian artisanal identity.

2020

3,000 Collectors

Despite global disruption, LUXWATCH grew its collector community to 3,000.

2025

The New Chapter

Our boldest collection yet: 47 timepieces across three lines, each numbered, each signed.

Our Values

What We Stand For
